## Overview

An analysis of [Jeff Sackmann's tennis dataset](https://github.com/JeffSackmann) cross-referenced against historical betting odds to find where markets consistently misprice match outcomes.

## Problem

Betting markets aggregate public perception and money flow — not necessarily ground truth probability. The hypothesis: there are identifiable match conditions where the implied probability from odds diverges systematically from actual outcomes.

## Overview

An interactive visualization tool for exploring sorting, pathfinding, and graph algorithms. Step through each operation in real time, inspect pseudocode, and track performance metrics as the algorithm runs.

## Instructions

Choose an algorithm from the sidebar, set your speed and array size, and hit play.
